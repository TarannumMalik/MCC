[Frequently Asked Questions]

What is MCC

Mumbai Coding Club offers a structured, flexible and guided learning program for software engineers to accelerate their career. This program is open for all software engineers irrespective of the # of years of work experience or their current place of work.

Our features are:

  1. structured curriculum that covers CS fundamentals via a project based approach. This curriculum has been industry-vetted and reverse-engineered by analyzing what it takes to be a great engineer.
  2. Offline classes that are held twice a week, with the timings deteminated by students in the batch.
  3. Tutors who have built scalables system in the past and understand the real-life importance of DSA, not just interview-relevant mterial.
  4. Quick doubt resolution from out Teaching Assistants.
  5. Flexible curriculum that help you continue learing despite your hectic work schedule. We provide recorded lectures to viiew later and also allow you to move between batches at NO cost.
  6. Career supprt - we provide job opportunities via mock interview services and career planning services.

Who is elligible for MCC

Sotware engineers with any number of years of experience can choose to join Mumbai Coding Club.Applicants currently in college(1st, 2nd, 3rd or final year of college) could consider to join Mumbai Coding Club, our college students focused product. please visitwww.mumbaicodingclub.com for more details.

When are the classes held?

Live classes are normally held 2 times a week, with the exact class days and timings being determined by the batch. However, given all learners would be software engineers that are currently working, the classes would be held in the late evening or night on weekdays and any suitable time over the weekends.

Can I enroll only into full-stack of the MCC?

No, we feel that before jumping into a full-stack module , you must learn the basics of programming, cs fundamentals, and Data Strauctures & Algorithms. These skills are essential to have before you can begin with a full-stack specialization.

Do all section start at the same time?

Yes, all sections start at the same time but end at different times, depending on the duration of the course for that section. The Advanced section ends in 9 months, the intermediate section ends in 11 months and the Beginner section ends in 13 months.
